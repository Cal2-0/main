About Calx Cipher

The Calx cipher is a sophisticated multi-layered encryption system that combines the strengths of multiple classical ciphers. It sequentially applies Caesar cipher shifts, Vigenère polyalphabetic substitution, and Base64 encoding to create a highly obfuscated result that's significantly more secure than any single cipher alone.
