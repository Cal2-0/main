Only The Chosen May Proceed

This is no ordinary journey.
The path has been scattered in whispers and shadows,
encoded beneath layers of quiet noise.
The key you seek is not written plainly,
but hidden throughout your quest.

To proceed, you must become the one who sees beyond.
The phrase to unlock your destiny
lies somewhere within the puzzle you have followed.
Seek it in silence.

When the time is right, type what you have uncovered —
the phrase — anywhere on this page.
The page is listening.
No box, no invitation.
Just your resolve.

Only then will the path reveal itself.
Only then will you become the chosen one.

(Hint: The key phrase was hidden in the puzzles you've solved.)
